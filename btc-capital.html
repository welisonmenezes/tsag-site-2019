DESCRIPTION

Crypto Structured Operations aimed at capturing different opportunity windows and customized to suit customers with different levels of risk tolerance. Possibility of leverage up to 100 times and protection of portion of the capital invested.

RISK AND REWARD PROFILE

The indicator measures the risk of price fluctuations in the portfolio based on the last 30d volatility. This means that the purchase of units in the portfolio is connected to medium risk of such fluctuations. Please note that category 1 does not mean a risk-free investment. Historical data, such as is used in calculating this indicator, may not be a reliable indication of the future risk profile of the Portfolio, why the category might change in the future.
